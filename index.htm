Ziphra Messenger 🔐

by Jorge Kagiagian

Why Ziphra?

Ziphra Messenger is more than just a secure app — it’s a statement. A declaration that your privacy is not a commodity, and your communication deserves absolute protection.

Designed for activists, journalists, developers, dissidents — and for anyone who simply values their freedom.

Security Features

🚫No data collection

🧹All metadata is stripped

🕵️‍♂️No person, organization, or government can access any information

🛡️User identities and contacts are fully protected

📶Works independently of any platform — even without a phone line

Storage & Communication

📭No messages, photos, audio, or videos are stored on the device

💬Communication is ephemeral, private, and fully secured

Open Source & Freedom

🧩Ziphra is 100% open source and licensed under the GNU General Public License v3.0 (GPLv3)

Anyone can audit, modify, or contribute to the project. There are no secrets — only strong encryption.
